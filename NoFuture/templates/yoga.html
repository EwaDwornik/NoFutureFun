I used to believe in the system of Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga. I've been practicing it for years, 5, 6 times a week, mostly at 6:00 AM. Sometimes it was hard, sometimes it was easy, but I did it anyway. I’ve practiced with few well-known, experienced teachers and also with a couple of less famous, but nonetheless knowledgeable ones. I’ve been to India three times, but to be honest I’ve learnt the most by being alone on my own mat, and applying regularly what I learnt in these journeys.

Recently I've taken a break, we will see what comes next.

I am a yoga teacher.

This is one of the fruits of my first trip to India, to Rishikesh - the world capital of yoga. I've gotten way more than this document, but fortunately we, as humanity, haven't started giving diplomas for that ;)
